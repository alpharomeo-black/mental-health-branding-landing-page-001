About the Client

Nicole Meier, ND

The realization of a dream, Cornerstone Health Centre is the brainchild of Nicole Meier, ND. Located in Georgetown, this ecological, complementary health clinic is a manifestation of Nicole's vision.

The practitioners were selected for their expertise across physical, emotional, and spiritual healthcare. The clinic features an ecological renovation and offers educational resources on detox, meditation, and emotional wellness.

The Challenge

The client sought a refreshed brand identity that honoured existing recognition while signaling growth. Rather than a complete redesign, the approach involved a strategic redesign — infusing the existing emblem with a fresh aesthetic.

This provided comfort and familiarity for existing clients alongside an evolution that would attract a new audience.
