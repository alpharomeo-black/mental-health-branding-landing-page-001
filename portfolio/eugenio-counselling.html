About the Client

Erica Eugenio, MSW RSW

Erica Eugenio is a Registered Social Worker specializing in grief counselling, family mediation, and youth support. Trained at Wilfrid Laurier University, she brings a compassionate, evidence-informed approach to her practice.

Through thoughtful therapeutic relationships, Erica helps clients navigate life's most difficult transitions — loss, conflict, and the complex emotional landscape of growing up.

Distilling Required

Built from Scratch

Erica came to us without any existing branding. She needed a complete visual identity created from the ground up — one that would reflect both the gravity of grief work and the warmth she brings to every session.

We developed a full brand system: logo, colour palette, typography, submarks, and brand patterns — giving her a cohesive identity ready to carry across every client touchpoint.
