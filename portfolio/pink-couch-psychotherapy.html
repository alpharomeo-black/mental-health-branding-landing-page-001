About the Client

Janice Daciuk

In the realm of Pink Couch Psychotherapy, Janice Daciuk extends a unique blend of therapy and personal, solution-focused life coaching tailored specifically for women aged 30 and above.

Offering her services in a home-based and down-to-earth setting, Janice creates a warm and approachable environment where clients feel at ease to explore and grow.

The Challenge

Having previously collaborated with Janice on her coaching practice, a new chapter unfolded as she transitioned into psychotherapy — necessitating a fresh look and brand for Pink Couch Psychotherapy.

The new brand needed to honour her playful personality while projecting the credibility of a licensed practitioner.
