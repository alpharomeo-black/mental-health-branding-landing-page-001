About the Client

Natalie Haynes, RP

Natalie is a Registered Psychotherapist with the College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario, as well as a member of The Ontario Association of Consultants, Counsellors, Psychometrists and Psychotherapists (OACCPP) and the Canadian Association for Psychodynamic Therapy (CAPT).

She attended the Transformational Arts College and was certified as a Spiritual Psychotherapist, then attended the Gestalt Institute of Toronto. Natalie's wish is not to "fix" or "change" her clients, but to assist them in gaining awareness in an honest and compassionate way.

Distilling Required

The Challenge

Natalie needed a re-brand with a focus on Gestalt therapy — a professional online presence true to her personality to showcase services, directed at her ideal audience.

She was also looking to build on her existing client base while establishing a stronger sense of credibility and warmth.
