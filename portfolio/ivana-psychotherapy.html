About the Client

Ivana Kolakovic, MA, RP

Ivana Kolakovic is a Registered Psychotherapist and trauma practitioner with a Master's degree. As a Registered Psychotherapist, she is deeply passionate about providing support in the process of acknowledging and articulating all that is painful and frightening within and around individuals.

Ivana completed a 5-year Psychotherapy training at the Gestalt Institute of Toronto and a 3-year Somatic Experiencing training for trauma resolution in Montreal. She also holds a Master's degree in Women's and Gender Studies from Carleton University.

The Challenge

In the quest for a rebranding initiative, Ivana sought to emphasize Gestalt therapy, crafting a professional online presence that authentically reflects her personality and showcases her services.

The primary target was her ideal audience, while concurrently aiming to expand upon her current client base.
